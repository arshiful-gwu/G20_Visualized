INTRODUCTION

VISUALIZATION OF G20 DATA

The Group of 20 is an organization of finance ministers and central bank governors from 19 individual countries and the European Union.

In addition to the United States, those countries are Argentina, Australia, Brazil, Britain, Canada, China, France, Germany, India, Indonesia, Italy, Japan, Mexico, Russia, Saudi Arabia, South Africa, South Korea and Turkey. Collectively, its members represent more than 80 percent of the world’s gross domestic product.

In this project we will be looking into the GDP, Healthcare, Military and Education Expenditure Data of these countries through data visualization techniques.

Through visualization we will compare:
The spending data in the healthcare, military and education industry to that country’s GDP
The education and health to the overall military spending of the country
The per person educational spending to the per person GDP
The per person healthcare spending to the per person GDP
The per person military spending to the per person GDP
And Identify the:
Fastest growing countries in healthcare and educational spending in fixed value and in percentage
Any interesting and useful relationships and trends
